## 🎯 Design Philosophy

The objective of the game is to demonstrate how everyday decisions can gradually shape a person's life.

Instead of obvious right-or-wrong answers, most choices involve trade-offs. Players must continuously balance mental health, finances, social relationships, and energy, reflecting the challenges faced by many university students.

The game encourages strategic thinking and highlights the long-term consequences of small daily decisions.
