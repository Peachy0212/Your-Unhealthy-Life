# 🎮 Your Unhealthy Life

A Reigns-inspired decision-making game where every choice affects your life.

Players take on the role of a university student trying to balance four important aspects of life while surviving 20 days of daily decisions.

---

## 📖 Overview

Every day, the player receives an event card and must choose between two options.

Each decision changes one or more life stats. If any stat reaches **0**, the game ends immediately.

Can you survive until **Day 20**?

---

## 🎯 Gameplay

Each turn:

1. Read the event.
2. Choose one of two actions.
3. Watch your stats change.
4. Continue to the next day.

The game ends when:

- Any stat reaches **0**
- OR you successfully survive until **Day 20**

---

## 📊 Stats

The player must balance four different attributes:

| Stat | Description |
|------|-------------|
| 🧠 Mental Health | Emotional and psychological wellbeing |
| 💰 Finance | Money available |
| 👥 Social | Relationships with family and friends |
| ⚡ Energy | Physical stamina |

Keeping every stat above zero is the key to survival.

---

## 🃏 Event System

The game contains a collection of random life events such as:

- Waking up late
- Part-time jobs
- Flash sales
- Deadlines
- Sports activities
- Social invitations
- Family phone calls
- Studying
- Entertainment

Some events only appear under specific conditions.

Examples:

- Burnout appears when Mental Health is very low.
- Investment opportunities appear when Finance is high.
- Social crisis appears when Social is too low.

This conditional event system makes every playthrough different.
